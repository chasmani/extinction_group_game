About The Study

The goal of this study was to understand how people reason about rare events with extreme consequences (the ‘extinction’ event). In particular, how participants' inclination to choose the risky option depends on 1) whether they are playing in a group or individually, 2) whether groups do better when voting together on actions or acting independently 3) how people respond to information about the possible outcomes of the game.
