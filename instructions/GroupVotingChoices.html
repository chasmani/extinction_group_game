NOTE TO MAX: ONLY SHOWN ON CONDITION WITH GROUP GAME WITH VOTING.

You play this game in a group of 5 players.

Each round, each player votes on whether to play the safe or the risky lottery.

The result of the vote determines which lottery all of the players play.

Each player plays the lottery on their own, and if they earn a bonus then that counts towards their own individual bonus.

If any player draws the extinction outcome from the risky lottery, the entire team loses the bonus payments for the whole experiment, and will not be able to earn any bonus.

Example: Safe Vote

In this example, 4 players have chosen the safe lottery and 1 player has chosen the risky lottery.

All 5 players will play the safe lottery this round.

Example: Risky Vote

In this example, 2 players have chosen the safe lottery and 3 players have chosen the risky lottery.

All 5 players will play the risky lottery this round.
