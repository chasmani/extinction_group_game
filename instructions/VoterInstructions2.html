The Group

You play this game in a group of 5 players.

As a group, each round you decide how many of the players play risky lotteries, and how many play safe.

Voting

Each round, each player votes on how many players, from 0 to 5, will play a risky lottery (instead of the safe lottery).

The votes of all the players are collected, sorted into order, and the middle value is chosen (examples below). That middle value determines how many players will be randomly selected to play the risky lottery

Group Outcomes

Once the voting is done, each player plays the lottery they are given on their own, and if they earn a bonus then that counts towards their own individual bonus.

IMPORTANT: If any player draws the extinction outcome from the risky lottery, the entire team loses the bonus payments for the whole experiment, and will not be able to earn any bonus.

Example 1

In this example, the players voted: 0,3,4,5,5.

The middle value is taken of 4, and so 4 randomly selected players in the group will play the risky lottery and the other player will play the safe lottery.

Example 2

In this example, the players voted: 0,0,1,1,4.

The middle value is taken of 1, and so 1 randomly selected player in the group will play the risky lottery and the other 4 players will play the safe lottery.
